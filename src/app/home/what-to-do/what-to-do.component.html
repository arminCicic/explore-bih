Buna channel

The Buna River is a stunning natural attraction located near Blagaj, Bosnia and Herzegovina. The river originates from a large underground karstic spring and flows into the Neretva River. One of the main highlights of the Buna River is the Buna Spring, which creates a picturesque turquoise pool surrounded by lush greenery.

Olympic Mountain Jahorina

Olympic Mountain Jahorina is a paradise for winter sports enthusiasts. Located in Bosnia and Herzegovina, it offers a wide range of winter activities such as skiing, snowboarding, and sledding. With over 20 kilometers of ski slopes, Jahorina is the perfect destination for both beginners and experienced skiers. Apart from winter sports, Jahorina also boasts breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and forest.

Wild horses in Livno

Livno is a beautiful region in Bosnia and Herzegovina where you can witness the stunning sight of wild horses running free. These magnificent creatures roam the vast wilderness of Livno and offer a unique experience for nature lovers and animal enthusiasts. Watching these wild horses gallop across the fields and hills is a truly breathtaking experience that will leave you in awe. The Livno wild horses are an important part of the region's natural heritage and a symbol of the area's untouched beauty
